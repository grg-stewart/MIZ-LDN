Oyster Card Tips
🚇 Pre-paid Oyster Card
Collected at hotel check-in on Tuesday. Covers Tube, buses, DLR, Elizabeth line and overground. Always tap IN and OUT at every station — if you forget to tap out you'll be charged the maximum fare. Top up at any Tube station if the balance runs low.
🐯 Tigers Assemble
Hard requirements
Any item marked 🐯 Tigers Assemble is a hard requirement — be at the specified location at the required time. For a performance, photocall, or a "use it or lose it" attraction ticket. The band bus waits for absolutely no one.

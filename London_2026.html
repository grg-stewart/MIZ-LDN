🎵 Gail — ABBA Voyage · 7:45 PM
ABBA Arena, 1 Pudding Mill Lane, E15 2RU. Show ~1hr 40min, no interval — out ~9:30 PM.

🚇 Option 1 — DLR (recommended):
Walk 5 min to Tower Gateway DLR. Board toward Stratford: Tower Gateway → Shadwell → Limehouse → Westferry → Poplar (change — step across platform, lift available) → All Saints → Langdon Park → Devons Road → Bow Church → Pudding Mill Lane. Arena directly across the street. ~18–20 min total.

🚕 Option 2 — Taxi/Uber:
~15–20 min direct to Pudding Mill Lane drop-off. East London Friday evening traffic is much lighter than the tourist areas — no comparison to midday near the Eye or Westminster.

Getting home (~9:30 PM):
🚇 DLR: Pudding Mill Lane station is right across the street from the arena exit. Board toward Tower Gateway: Pudding Mill Lane → Bow Church → Devons Road → Langdon Park → All Saints → Poplar (change) → Westferry → Limehouse → Shadwell → Tower Gateway. Walk 5 min to hotel. ~18–20 min.
🚕 Taxi/Uber from Pudding Mill Lane outside the arena — ~15 min at 9:30 PM with light traffic.
🚇 Wembley transit — Katie · Alicia · Lily
Getting there:
Step 1 — Katie & Alicia: Tower Hill → Green Park (District/Circle, ~20 min), then Green Park → Piccadilly Circus (Piccadilly line, 1 stop, 2 min). Meet Lily at Zedwell Piccadilly.
Step 2 — All three: Piccadilly Circus → Green Park (Piccadilly line, 2 min) → Wembley Park (Jubilee line direct, ~22 min).
Doors open 5:00 PM but opening act isn't until 6:45–7:00 PM — depart hotel by 4:30 PM, arrive Wembley ~5:45 PM.

Getting home (after ~10:30–11 PM):
Don't rush — wait 15–20 min inside or nearby to let the post-show surge clear (Wembley Park queues can be 30–60 min immediately after). Use Empire Way (turn left out of stadium, behind OVO Arena) rather than Olympic Way to reach Wembley Park station with fewer crowds.
All three: Wembley Park → Piccadilly Circus (Jubilee line to Green Park, change to Piccadilly line, ~25 min — Jubilee runs 24hrs on Fridays).
